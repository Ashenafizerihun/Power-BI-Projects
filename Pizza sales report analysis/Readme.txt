Pizza Sales Data Analysis üçï

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of pizza sales data using **SQL** for data querying and **Power BI** for visualization. The goal was to uncover key insights into sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance.


Project Components üìä

  SQL Queries: The `PIZZA SALES SQL QUERIES.docx` file contains all the SQL queries used to extract and prepare the data for analysis. The queries cover key performance indicators (KPIs) and various trends.
  * **Power BI Reports**: The `Pizza_sales_report.pdf` file showcases the final reports and visualizations. This includes dashboards for daily and monthly order trends, sales by pizza category and size, and best/worst-performing pizzas.

-----

### Key Findings and Insights üìà

The analysis revealed several important trends and performance metrics:

  * **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**:

      Total Revenue: The total revenue generated from pizza sales was **$817,860.05**
      Total Pizzas Sold: A total of **49,574** pizzas were sold
      Total Orders: The total number of orders placed was **21,350**
      Average Order Value: The average value of each order was approximately **$38.31**
      Average Pizzas Per Order: The average number of pizzas per order was **2.32**

  **Sales Trends**:

      Daily Trends: Fridays had the highest number of orders, with **3,538**, while Sundays had the     lowest at **2,624**
      Monthly Trends: July saw the highest number of orders at **1,935**, and October had the lowest with **1,646**

  **Performance by Category and Size**:

      Pizza Category: The **Classic** pizza category accounted for the largest percentage of sales at **26.91%**, followed by Supreme at **25.46%**
      Pizza Size: **Large** pizzas were the most popular, making up **45.89%** of the total revenue

  **Best and Worst Sellers**:

      Top 5 by Revenue: "The Thai Chicken Pizza" led the top 5 pizzas by revenue with **$43,434.25**
      Bottom 5 by Revenue: "The Brie Carre Pizza" was at the bottom with **$11,588.50** in total revenue
      Top 5 by Quantity: "The Classic Deluxe Pizza" was the most sold pizza with **2,453** units
      Bottom 5 by Quantity: "The Brie Carre Pizza" was the least sold, with only **490** units       
      Top 5 by Total Orders: "The Classic Deluxe Pizza" also had the highest number of orders, at **2,329**
